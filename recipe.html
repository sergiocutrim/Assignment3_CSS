Recipe

Try this easy no-churn ice cream recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract.
  2. In another bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  3. Fold the whipped cream into the condensed milk mixture until well combined.
  4. Divide the mixture in half. To one half, add the cocoa powder and mix well. Stir in chocolate chips if desired.
  5. Transfer the chocolate mixture to a freezer-safe container. Spoon the vanilla mixture on top and swirl gently with a knife.
  6. Cover and freeze for at least 6 hours or until firm.
  7. Scoop and enjoy your homemade no-churn mint chocolate ice cream!
